About Louise

I am a City & Guilds and British Academy of Interior Design trained freelance interior designer. Having previously worked as a Home Design Stylist for John Lewis for over three years, I have designed hundreds of unique room schemes for a wide range of clients and budgets, including holiday rentals, city apartments and large rural properties. I always begin designing with layout, as practicality is essential, and then create warm, comfortable, inspiring schemes which my clients love to live in. I don?t have a signature style as I always design for the client, but I do like to encourage my clients to step out of their comfort zone and work with them to create beautiful, engaging spaces which they adore.

I have a background in Theatre Design, but retrained in Interior Design with City and Guilds and the British Academy of Interior Design. Why choose me?

I can look at your project with a fresh pair of eyes and because I am an independent designer, I can give unbiased advice and offer solutions without trying to sell you products.

I have over three years worth of experience as a designer for John Lewis, and more as a freelance designer. I have designed hundreds of schemes and tackled most room/building types and the challenges they present. I have worked with clients of all tastes and budgets, and pride myself on getting to know the client and their needs, and provide schemes which exceed their expectations.
